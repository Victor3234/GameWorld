    About Game

    The game takes place in the late 13th century on Tsushima Island, which serves as the initial frontline of the Mongol invasion of Japan. Players are immersed in a meticulously crafted and visually striking representation of feudal Japan, complete with lush landscapes, serene forests, and historically accurate architecture. The island's environment plays a significant role in both the narrative and gameplay, providing opportunities for exploration and discovery.
