    About Game

    Diablo 4 continues the series' tradition of taking place in the dark and gothic world of Sanctuary. The game is set decades after the events of Diablo 3 and explores the aftermath of the destruction caused by the Prime Evils. Players will journey through various regions, including the dry deserts of Kejhistan, the eerie swamplands of Scosglen, and the foreboding wilderness of Hawezar.
