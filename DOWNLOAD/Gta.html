    About Game

    "Grand Theft Auto V" (GTA 5) is an action-adventure game that offers both a third-person and first-person perspective to players. In the game, players complete various missions with set objectives to advance through the story, and when not on missions, they have the freedom to explore the expansive open world. The game is set in the fictional, Los Angeles-inspired city of Los Santos, which is rich in detail and populated by a variety of characters. The narrative is complex, featuring a colorful cast of characters, double-crosses, botched film productions, and deep government conspiracies The storyline centers around three protagonists: Franklin, a street hustler seeking genuine opportunities; Michael, a retired professional criminal; and Trevor, a wild card character whose actions are unpredictable. Their lives intertwine as they plan and execute a series of heists to secure their future in a city where they can trust nobody, least of all each other
