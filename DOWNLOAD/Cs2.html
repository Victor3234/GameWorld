    About Game

    CS2 features two opposing teams, the Terrorists (T) and the Counter-Terrorists (CT), each with their distinct objectives and roles. The primary game modes include Bomb Defusal, Hostage Rescue, and Arms Race, with Bomb Defusal being the most iconic and widely played. In Bomb Defusal, the Terrorists must plant a bomb at one of two designated sites (A or B), while the Counter-Terrorists aim to prevent the bomb from being planted or defuse it if it's already planted. The game emphasizes team coordination, strategy, and individual skill, with players selecting from a wide array of weapons and equipment. CS2 competitive nature is characterized by its economy system, where players earn money for kills, objectives, and round wins, which they can use to purchase weapons, armor, and grenades. Effective resource management is crucial for success, as it can determine a team's overall strategy and tactics.
