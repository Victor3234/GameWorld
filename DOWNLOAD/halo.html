    About Game

    Halo Infinite takes place on a new installation called "Installation 07" or simply "Zeta Halo." This mysterious Halo ringworld is vast and offers a diverse range of environments for players to explore, from lush forests to expansive landscapes. Chief Returns: The game marks the return of Master Chief, the series' iconic protagonist, who finds himself stranded on Zeta Halo after the events of Halo 5: Guardians. Players will once again take on the role of the Spartan super-soldier. Gameplay: Halo Infinite aims to recapture the essence of the earlier games in the series, focusing on the sandbox-style gameplay that allows players to approach encounters with a wide variety of weapons, vehicles, and equipment. Classic Halo weapons and vehicles will make a return, along with new additions.
